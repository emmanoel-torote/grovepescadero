About the Community

Where the farmland
meets the Pacific

Nestled between the fertile farmlands and pristine coastline of Baja California Sur, The Grove at Pescadero is a curated community of 18 private residences built around a singular vision: luxury in harmony with the land.

Each home is designed by a visionary architect-builder to embody regenerative living — where modern comforts, ocean views, and a connection to the surrounding agricultural landscape create a daily experience unlike anywhere else in Baja.

Minutes from world-class surf breaks, an innovative culinary scene, and the cultural richness of Todos Santos, The Grove offers a lifestyle as rare as its location.

The Lifestyle

Designed for how you live

Farm-to-Table Living

The community is woven into the agricultural landscape of Pescadero — farmlands at your doorstep, the freshest produce as a daily rhythm. Living here means eating differently, and living better.

Surf, Sea & Solitude

Minutes from world-class surf breaks and the Pacific coast, The Grove gives you immediate access to Baja's extraordinary natural playground — without sacrificing the comforts of an architecturally designed home.

Regenerative Design

Every architectural decision — from materials to orientation to landscape — is made in service of ecological balance. The Grove was conceived not to interrupt the land, but to participate in it.
